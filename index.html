About Lifestyle Medicine

Lifestyle medicine is an evidence-based approach to preventing, treating, and reversing chronic diseases by addressing their root causes through lifestyle changes. The six pillars of lifestyle medicine include:

  • Healthy Eating
  • Regular Physical Activity
  • Restorative Sleep
  • Stress Management
  • Avoidance of Risky Substances
  • Positive Social Connection
